Little Forest, Little Wish

I recorded 4 seasons for the trees standing in front of my house since last year. I name this the same as the Japanese film "Little Forest", which is also a recording of 4 seasons based on a girl's life. I feel like this is my version of "Little Forest". I particularly enjoyed the 4 series of the film, but I would venture to guess that a hothead would not have liked such a slow play. The speed with which modern people receive information, think and change is too fast. Too many people have lost the ability to feel the world with their heart, and so did I a few years ago. Over the course of a year documenting these photographs, I began to become more aware of the subtle changes that had taken place around me that I had previously overlooked. I may not stay in the same place for a long time anymore, recording everything that changes silently, but I will always try to feel, rather than forget and ignore.
